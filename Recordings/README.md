### GameplayDemo_01.mp4
This recording shows a short level on the easiest difficulty. This is the simple gameplay loop, where the user can upgrade their towers and keep enemies from exiting the map via the red cube. The enemies shown here include:

Basic enemy: The blue ball, has no outstanding characteristics.

Speedy enemy: The smaller yellow ball, has less health than the basic enemy but is significantly faster.

Tough enemy: The larger orange ball, has more health and less speed than the basic enemy. Also takes more power to punch through.

Ghost enemy: The white ball, this enemy remains a threat for a short while after its health is depleted. It is important to kill this enemy early in the track, so it doesn't exit before it disappears.

Speedy boss: The larger yellow ball, this enemy is the boss version of the Speedy enemy. It just has more health.

Basic boss: The large red ball, this enemy has a ton of health and pretty low speed.

Here is a youtube link if that is preferable: https://youtu.be/jtgY2oxjKlo
