# Screenshots

### Gameplay1.png
This image shows a simple gameplay moment. A turret has been selected by the user, and the turret's relevant UI is displayed. The dark circle around the turret is its range of enemy detection. The bright pink
area is where turrets are allowed to be placed. Enemies are the blue spheres, which come out of the green cube and exit through the red cube. They follow the dark red path.

---

### Gameplay2.png
This image shows another gameplay moment, where the player upgraded a turret. The white cubes shown on the turret model are placeholder models for when I implement actual models for each upgrade. 
The money is intended to be negative as a part of an unimplemented risk/reward system, where the player may upgrade a turret early but suffer the consequences of being in debt, such as a "loan shark"
boss enemy appearing if the player is too deep in debt, or accumulating interest that forces the player to remain in debt longer.
